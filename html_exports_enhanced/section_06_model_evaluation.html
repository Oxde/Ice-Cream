6 Model Evaluation

6.1 Evaluation Methodology

To ensure realistic evaluation, we applied a temporal train/test split with 129 weeks (83%) for training and 27 weeks (17%) for testing. This maintains chronological order, eliminating data leakage.

We employed Ridge regression with L2 regularization to handle multicollinearity while maintaining stable coefficient estimates. Ridge regression trades traditional p-value significance testing for improved prediction accuracy.

Note on Statistical Significance: Ridge regression does not provide traditional p-values due to regularization bias. Instead, we assess feature importance through coefficient magnitudes and business interpretability.
